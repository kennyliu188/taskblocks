# Privacy Policy for Task Blocks

**Last updated:** [15th,March,2026]

## Overview

Task Blocks ("we", "our", or "the app") is a task management application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

## Data We Collect

### Data Stored on Your Device
- **Task data**: Your tasks, categories, and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not upload this data to our servers.
- This data remains under your control and is not transmitted to us.

### Data Collected by Third-Party Services
- **Advertising**: The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device information, advertising IDs, and interaction data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- We do not control or access the data collected by AdMob.

## How We Use Information

- Task data is used only within the app to provide task management features.
- We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.
- AdMob uses collected data to show relevant ads and measure ad performance.

## Data Security

Your task data is stored locally on your device. We recommend using device security (e.g., lock screen) to protect your data.

## Children's Privacy

Task Blocks is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If your app targets children, additional disclosures may be required.

## Changes to This Policy
